Utah begins to mail ballots to every active registered voter 21 days before the election. Keep an eye on the mailbox! Be sure to mail it back so that it is postmarked no later than the day before Election Day, or return it to a vote center or drop box before 8 p.m. on Election Day. You can also vote in person if you prefer to do so. Utah offers early voting.
